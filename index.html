About Bengaluru City Hospital

Comprehensive Care for Bengaluru Urban.

Located in the heart of BENGALURU URBAN, 560078, Bengaluru City Hospital is more than just a hospital — it is a trusted partner in health and wellness. We deliver comprehensive care that addresses both physical and emotional well-being.

With state-of-the-art facilities, a highly skilled team of medical professionals and a patient-centric approach, we provide personalised attention for everything from routine check-ups to advanced surgical procedures and emergency care.

From general medicine and pediatrics to specialised care in cardiology, orthopedics and neurology, our integration of cutting-edge technology with evidence-based practice ensures high-quality outcomes and a comforting experience for patients and families.

By offering affordable, high-quality medical services and community health initiatives, Bengaluru City Hospital continues to strengthen healthcare accessibility and trust across Bengaluru Urban.
